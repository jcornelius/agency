# Square POS Prospect Research

*Research Date: March 2, 2026*

## Market Position & Overview

**Square's Market Share:** 28.10% (Leading position in POS Systems market)

**Top Competitors:**
- Toast: 23.29% market share (restaurant-focused)
- Retail Sales Manager: 11.02%
- Clover: 6.46%
- Lightspeed, PayPal POS, Shopify POS

## Square's Pricing Model (2026)

**Software Plans:**
- **Free Tier:** Basic POS functionality
- **Square Plus:** $49/month per location (staff management, marketing, loyalty)
- **Processing Fees:** Flat-rate model (becomes expensive as sales increase)

**Hardware:**
- **Square Register:** $799 upfront or $39/month for 24 months
- **Entry-level options:** Starting at $29/month

## Target Customer Segments

**Primary:** Small businesses, startups, mobile sellers
**Secondary:** Retail shops, cafés, food trucks, salons

## Key Pain Points & Limitations

### 1. **Scalability Issues**
- Flat-rate processing becomes expensive as sales volume increases
- Many businesses outgrow Square's capabilities as they expand
- Limited customization for growing businesses

### 2. **Industry-Specific Limitations**
- **Restaurants:** Toast offers superior restaurant-specific features
- **Enterprise:** Lacks advanced features needed by larger operations
- **Complex Operations:** Limited compared to specialized industry solutions

### 3. **Cost Structure**
- Processing fees become prohibitive at higher volumes
- Competitors offer interchange-plus pricing that scales better
- Hardware costs can add up quickly

## Prospecting Opportunities

### High-Value Prospects

#### 1. **Growing Square Users (Prime Targets)**
- **Profile:** Businesses doing >$50K/month in sales on Square
- **Pain Point:** Rising processing costs, outgrowing features
- **Solution Angle:** Cost savings + advanced features

#### 2. **Restaurant Owners on Square**
- **Profile:** Sit-down restaurants, bars, fast-casual chains
- **Pain Point:** Limited restaurant-specific functionality
- **Alternative:** Toast, Aloha Cloud, specialized restaurant POS
- **Solution Angle:** Industry-specific features, better table management

#### 3. **Multi-Location Retailers**
- **Profile:** Businesses with 3+ locations using Square
- **Pain Point:** Limited enterprise features, reporting limitations
- **Solution Angle:** Centralized management, advanced analytics

#### 4. **High-Transaction Volume Businesses**
- **Profile:** Businesses processing >$10K daily transactions
- **Pain Point:** Flat-rate processing fees eating profits
- **Solution Angle:** Interchange-plus pricing models

### Competitive Positioning Against Square

#### **When to Target Square Users:**
1. **Cost concerns** - Processing fees becoming prohibitive
2. **Feature limitations** - Need industry-specific tools
3. **Scalability** - Outgrowing Square's capabilities
4. **Integration needs** - Require deeper third-party integrations
5. **Customer support** - Seeking more personalized service

#### **Red Flags (Don't Target):**
- Very small businesses (<$5K monthly sales)
- Businesses happy with simplicity
- New startups just getting started
- Mobile-only operations (Square's strength)

## Prospect Research Strategy
